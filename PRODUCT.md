# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Muslim and Christian entrepreneurs, startup founders, and investors — primarily in the Web3, crypto, real estate, and emerging tech space. They're community-oriented and faith-conscious, looking for both capital connections and peer networks.

## Product Purpose

Project Blue is a funding and networking platform that connects entrepreneurs with investors and collaborators. It positions itself as the go-to hub for startup funding, networking events, and investment opportunities — with a specific embrace of Web3, NFTs, real estate, and emerging tech. Success looks like a prospective member hitting the page and immediately hitting "Secure your spot now."

## Brand Personality

Bold · Visionary · Community

## Anti-references

- Generic fintech (navy + gold) — stiff, institutional, old-money
- Plain startup landing page — Notion-template, SaaS-cream, safe
- Religious / mosque aesthetic — no traditional Islamic geometry, calligraphy, or mosque imagery as the visual language

## Design Principles

1. **Web3-native confidence** — The aesthetic should feel like it belongs at the frontier of the internet, not a retrofit of a traditional finance page.
2. **Community before product** — The platform is a people network first; the design should communicate warmth and belonging inside the futuristic shell.
3. **Earn the ambition** — Every bold visual choice (neon, scale, motion) should feel intentional and legible, not decoration for its own sake.
4. **Signal trust fast** — Investors and founders scan for credibility signals; the page must communicate legitimacy (real members, real funding amounts, real partners) within the first fold.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Audience is diverse and global; no assumptions about language, device, or ability. Reduced-motion support required for the marquee and any scroll animations.
